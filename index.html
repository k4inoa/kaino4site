 Kaino4 Studios respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Minesweepah and Drummakid handle information.

Information We Collect
We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal information through Minesweepah or Drummakid.

Local Device Storage
These apps may store settings, saved state, high scores, or similar app information locally on your device only to support normal app functionality.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking
We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies in these apps.

Accounts
Our apps do not require you to create an account.

Children's Privacy
Our apps are not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services
If a future version of either app adds third-party services or changes how information is handled, we will update this Privacy Policy before or when those changes take effect.

Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@kaino4apps.store.
